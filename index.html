I'm Parth — an editor, cinematographer, and colorist working with clients across the world. Four years in, and what still drives me is the same thing it always was: a film that actually has something to say.

I'm drawn to story-based work — the kind that challenges me creatively, where the edit isn't just assembly but a decision about how an audience feels at every cut. Whether I'm shooting, grading, or building a sequence in the timeline, I bring the same approach: clean, intentional, and built around what the story needs.

I work with premium brands, independent filmmakers, and anyone serious about the quality of what they put out into the world.
